# Underground Biomes Forged

> Reimagining Minecraft underground generation with massive geological diversity.

---

## Overview

Underground Biomes Forged transforms Minecraft's underground world into a more immersive and geological experience.

Instead of endless layers of identical stone, the underground becomes a diverse environment consisting of multiple rock regions, natural transitions, underground variation and improved exploration possibilities.

Designed for modpacks and long-term worlds.

---

## Features

### Geological Underground Generation
- Multiple underground stone regions
- Distinct geological zones
- Natural transitions between rock layers

### World Integration
- Compatible with Minecraft world generation
- Built for Forge environments
- Modpack friendly

### Immersion Focus
- Improved cave identity
- Exploration becomes more meaningful
- Better visual variety underground

### Extensible Design
- Configuration-driven systems
- Future datapack compatibility
- Designed for additional biome integration
